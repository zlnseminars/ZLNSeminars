The Crown: The Jewel of Jealousy-Part Four

The thing about life is that we experience both the good and the bad. This duality teaches us balance. It teaches us how to balance the weight by leaning on our bad experiences to gain the strength to guard our fine and authentic jewels. Counterfeit jewels seem to “lighten” the weight. They don’t. If anything they add more weight because they blind us to the truth of our own pain. We must guard our crowns at all costs. No matter how hard life gets, we must guard them. Guard your love, your joy ,and peace; guard your self control. Guard them.

Truth be told our crowns are heavy. It is the pressure of carrying the crown that leads to the exchange of the authentic for the counterfeit to “lighten” the load. Missing any of our authentic jewels causes an imbalance. It’s not easy being us. Our lives are not all that those who covet it make it out to be. Yes, we are blessed and have blessings raining down all around us, but we also have trials to endure. Dealing with the jealousy of others is hard. To deal with their persecution makes our guarding our crowns even harder.

But, we can handle it. Understanding is key to balancing the weight. I can balance and therefore manage the weight of my crown in addition to the persecution of those who I thought had my back because I recognize that I once was them.

Acting out of anger and hurt is how we lose our fine authentic jewels. We must act out of love and understanding. It’s not easy. I’m not saying that we are responsible for helping them balance their crowns and retrieve their fine jewels again. I’m saying that to maintain and guard our own, we must remember how we used to be and tap into that understanding; send them love and light and carry on in wearing our priceless crowns, knowing that eventually they’ll come around to find the value of their crowns too.

No matter how deep the offense, guard your crown. Don’t give in to the pressures of the counterfeits. We all have weight to bear. It’s that level of understanding that keeps the weight of the crown in balance. So keep wearing those crowns my fellow royals and continue to guard your fine, authentic jewels.

You are priceless!
